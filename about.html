A little about me

I'm Ashok Kumar Rathod, a UX Designer with a Master's in Interaction and Experience Design from the University of Limerick, Ireland, and a Bachelor's in Computer Science. My approach to design is deeply rooted in human-centred thinking — I believe great products are built by first understanding the people who use them: their goals, behaviours, frustrations, and mental models.

My UX practice spans the full design lifecycle. On the research side, I'm experienced in user interviews, contextual enquiry, ethnographic research, empathy mapping, personas, heuristic evaluation, and usability testing — methods that help me uncover genuine user needs rather than assumptions. On the design side, I translate those insights into wireframes, interaction flows, prototypes, and polished UI using tools like Figma and Adobe XD, iterating continuously based on real feedback.

I also bring a strong technical foundation from my time as an Application Developer at Infosys, where I worked with clients across financial services, insurance, and retail (including Walmart). Working with Angular, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript gave me a practical understanding of frontend systems and technical constraints — which means I design with feasibility in mind and communicate more effectively with engineering teams. This combination of research rigour, interaction design craft, and technical fluency is what I bring to every project. I'm comfortable operating across discovery and delivery — facilitating workshops, synthesising insights, and shipping designs that are both user-centred and technically grounded.
