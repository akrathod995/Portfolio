My Audacity Project Procedure:

Please find my sound story here.

Click on the play button to hear my story

The sound story here is made by mixing various tracks in a way to narrate a story without any visual or wriitten understanding.

  1. In the Beginning , I googled over the internet for some audio samples to make use of them in my project. I have extracted most of the audio samples from various online resources. The following are the list of audio samples that I made use of in my project
    • Grunt, groan, lifting, strain, female, labor pains, pregnant, human voice - Sound Effects
    • Getting in car, Car on road,Car Crash, Traffic Hornking, Ambulance Sounds
    • Rain Sounds, Stretcher, Door Opening and Closing
    • Man Breathing, Woman Breathing, Man Crying, Baby Crying
    • Hartbeat, Heartbeat Flat sound effect, Piano sound, Sad Violin
  2. Normalized tracks in Audacity.
  3. Used the envelope tool to create a variation throughout and reduced the sound wherever I thought should be less heard
  4. Trimmed , Aligned and Faded car sounds
  5. Also, there are sections in between multiple tracks that i left to create a void effect so that it looks more realistic and silencing. For example, after the crash
  6. Merged Rain Sounds to Ambulance honking. Made use of echo effect to give the thundering sound a bit more depth. Lastly used fade out feature to lessen the intensity
  7. Used the envelope tool to reduce the intensity of the tracks
  8. I aligned the horn track with the rain to make it even more interesting. I used fade out effect in the end for the voice to lessen so that the ambulance approaching can be heard.
  9. For the ambulance track , I made use of both Fade in and Fade out effects including the use of the envelope tool varying its volume.
  10. The stretcher track I had was very short. So, I made use of repeat effect and set it to 1.
  11. I have aligned with it the man breathing track and the door opening and closing track to give a serious effect.
  12. I then made use of Repeat effect again for two of my heartbeat tracks. To the baby cry track , I used repeat effect and I made use of envelope tool to bring variations in its volume, level by level in an descending order so that it is lightly heard behind the scene throughout until needed.
  13. I have made use of the piano track to create a sense and effect of loss as to something terrible happened.
  14. I made use of trim option to cut the man crying track. Selected a part in it and did a repeat to it. The sad violin track plays alongside with the man crying which I thought would create a sad effect . I used the envelope tool again to bring in variations in the volume. I used fade out effect to its end.
  15. I have made use of the burning fire soundtrack in the end with the fade out option again.Re-Aligned tracks again and checked to see if the transitioning of tracks sound good. Saved the project and then exported it as an MP3
